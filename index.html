FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

We understand you may have some extra questions.

How is my child's safety ensured during the residency?+
Safety is our top priority. Our accommodation is secured 24/7 with staff supervision at all times. Medical support is always available, and all players follow clear routines and rules to minimise risks. Parents are kept fully informed throughout the process.
What does a typical day look like for the players?+
Each day combines training, education, and personal development. Mornings begin with training, followed by breakfast and academic or language lessons. Afternoon sessions include additional training and recovery time. The day concludes with team activities and individual coaching sessions.
What happens if my child becomes ill or gets injured?+
We have medical staff on-site at all times. Coaches and physiotherapists guide the recovery process, and parents are immediately informed of any health concerns. We also have partnerships with local hospitals and specialists for additional care if needed.
How can my child stay in contact with us during their stay?+
We encourage regular contact through video and phone calls. Players have access to secure communication channels, and parents receive scheduled updates about training progress and any important notes from staff.
What are the exact costs of the program?+
The program starts at €2,400 per month. Final pricing depends on the length of stay and the specific package chosen. During your admission or consultation meeting, you will receive detailed information about all costs and payment options available.
What is the minimum age to join?+
The minimum age to join the FK Riteriai Residency Program is 16 years old. Players must demonstrate strong technical and athletic ability, assessed via video submission or a live trial at the club.
